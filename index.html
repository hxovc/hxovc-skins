📅 Development Timeline

Kasumi V1.4 (2024)

Debut of AegleWorks. Full support for all game modes, illustrated mod icons and redesigned pause menu.

Kasumi V1.3 (2023)

First stable version of the Kasumi series. Clean design focused on osu!standard.

Pre-Kasumi V1.2.5 (2023)

Early prototype that led to the Kasumi line. Experimental version without finishing touches.

Experimentation (2022-2023)

Period of using and modifying popular skins like Rafis, Aristia and others.
